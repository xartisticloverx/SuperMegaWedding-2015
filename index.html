Our Story

“Hello there. Have any recommendations on good FBI books? FBI profiling is something I've always had an interest in, but never done much reading on. I must change this. Also, for what it's worth, I give your musical choices a double thumbs up!”

— Muzikale

Spring 2011, there was still a left over chill in the air from a fairly mild Ohio winter.

The location: Rancho Allegre. Quesadillas were eaten, margaritas drank, and (unknowingly) the foundation laid for a love of the ages.

After an initial string of conversations about everything from FBI profiling to puppy herding and the fine art of naps, we started to hang out and find that we had even more in common than expected.

Date one led to date two, and three, and before we knew it, we were discussing moving in together.
With the addition of another puppy, we knew we were in it for the long haul.

After a few months of Kyle’s foot dragging, he finally took a deep breath and anxiously dropped to one knee. Since you’re reading this on the site for our upcoming wedding; you can see how that one turned out!
